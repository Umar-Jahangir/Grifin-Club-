The Griffin Club VIT Pune

About Griffin

Griffin is a club at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune focused on AERO DESIGNS. Our skilled aeronautics team crafts aircraft from scratch and competes among the nation's top teams in the SAE Aero Design Challenges.

Why Aeronautical Engineering?

We believe every young enthusiast can create something innovative. Griffin provides the foundation to explore uncharted territories and elevate your model to new heights.

How We Do It

By combining innovation, passion, and expertise, we design and fabricate extraordinary aerial systems, testing their limits to achieve unparalleled success in competitions and research.
